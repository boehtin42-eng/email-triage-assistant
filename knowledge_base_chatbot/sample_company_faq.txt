Company FAQ and Service Policy

Company Name: Bright Cleaning Service

1. Services
Bright Cleaning Service provides home cleaning, office cleaning, deep cleaning, and monthly cleaning packages.

2. Business Hours
Our business hours are Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
We are closed on Sunday and public holidays.

3. Booking Policy
Customers can book a cleaning appointment by phone, WhatsApp, email, or website form.
For normal cleaning, customers should book at least 24 hours before the preferred appointment time.
For deep cleaning, customers should book at least 48 hours in advance.

4. Cancellation Policy
Customers can cancel or reschedule an appointment for free if they contact us at least 24 hours before the appointment.
If the customer cancels less than 24 hours before the appointment, a cancellation fee may apply.

5. Monthly Cleaning Package
The monthly cleaning package is recommended for customers who need regular cleaning every week or every two weeks.
The price depends on the size of the home or office, the number of rooms, and the cleaning frequency.

6. Payment Policy
Customers can pay by cash, bank transfer, or online payment.
Payment is usually collected after the service is completed.
For monthly packages, payment can be made monthly in advance.

7. Customer Support
If a customer has a complaint, the support team should respond politely and ask for the service date, customer name, and problem details.
Urgent complaints should be marked as high priority and sent to the manager.

8. Refund Policy
Refunds are reviewed case by case.
If the company made a clear service mistake, the customer may receive a partial refund or a free correction visit.

9. Staff Instructions
Staff should arrive on time, wear clean uniforms, speak politely, and take before/after photos when required.
If the customer requests extra work that is not included in the booking, staff should contact the office before agreeing.

10. Common Customer Questions
Question: Do you provide cleaning supplies?
Answer: Yes, we can bring cleaning supplies. Customers can also request us to use their preferred products.

Question: Can I book for tomorrow?
Answer: Yes, if there is availability. For normal cleaning, booking at least 24 hours in advance is recommended.

Question: How much is the monthly package?
Answer: The price depends on the home or office size and cleaning frequency. Please send the address, number of rooms, and preferred schedule for a quote.
